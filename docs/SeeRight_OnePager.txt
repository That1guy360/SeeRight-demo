SeeRight — Your prescription, in every screen. (Patent-pending)

SeeRight converts any digital screen into a personalized corrective display by applying real-time GPU distortion driven by a user's prescription (sphere, cylinder, axis). With AI-powered blur detection and an accessible calibration flow, SeeRight offers glasses-free clarity for phones, tablets, laptops, and future AR devices. Deployable as an installable PWA and native packages—ideal for consumers and OEM licensing.

Key Features:
- Prescription-driven shader correction (myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism)
- AI blur detection and auto-suggestion
- Tap-to-align calibration and voice guidance
- PWA + native packaging for OEM licensing
